Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how OnlineQrs protects your data through client-side processing and our commitment to never collecting personal information.

No Data Collection Client-Side Processing No Tracking No Third-Party Sharing

Executive Summary

OnlineQrs operates on a fundamentally different privacy model than most online services. All QR code generation happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device, and we never collect, store, or analyze any personal information.

What We Don't Do

  • Don't collect personal information
  • Don't store QR code content
  • Don't track user activity
  • Don't use analytics cookies
  • Don't share data with third parties

What We Do

  • Process everything in your browser
  • Use HTTPS for secure connections
  • Clear data after each session
  • Maintain transparency about practices
  • Regularly audit our security measures

1. Data Collection: Zero Collection Policy

OnlineQrs operates on a strict zero-data-collection policy. Unlike traditional web services that process data on their servers, we've designed our platform to work entirely within your browser.

What This Means for You:

Client-Side Processing

When you enter content to generate a QR code, that information is processed locally by JavaScript running in your browser. The data never travels over the internet to our servers.

No Server Storage

We don't have databases storing user information. There's no way for us to retain or access the content you enter into our QR code generator.

2. Technical Architecture: Client-Side Processing

Understanding our technical approach helps explain why we can guarantee your privacy:

Step 1: Input in Browser

You enter content (URL, text, contact info) into the QR code generator interface in your web browser.

Step 2: Local Processing

JavaScript libraries in your browser process the input and generate the QR code image locally on your device.

Step 3: Direct Download

The generated QR code is available for immediate download without any data transmission to external servers.

Key Technical Difference

Traditional QR code generators send your data to their servers for processing. OnlineQrs performs all processing locally using JavaScript. This architectural difference is fundamental to our privacy guarantees.

Our Cookie Policy in Detail:

  • Theme Preference Cookie: If you enable dark/light mode, a simple cookie stores this preference locally. This cookie contains no personal information and cannot be used to track you across websites.
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We don't integrate third-party services that would place cookies on your device.
  • Automatic Clearing: Any temporary data generated during QR code creation is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab.
  • Browser Storage: We may use localStorage (a browser feature) to store temporary preferences, but this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to us.
Important Note About Third-Party Services

While OnlineQrs itself doesn't use tracking cookies, your browser or internet service provider may collect data independently. We encourage you to review your browser's privacy settings and consider using privacy-focused browsers for maximum protection.

4. Data Security Measures

Although we don't collect data, we still implement robust security measures to protect our platform and users:

HTTPS Encryption

All connections to OnlineQrs.com are encrypted using TLS (Transport Layer Security) 1.2 or higher. This ensures that even basic connection metadata is protected.

Regular Security Audits

We conduct regular security assessments of our codebase and infrastructure to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

Vulnerability Management

We maintain a responsible disclosure policy and promptly address any reported security issues.

Secure Code Practices

Our development follows security best practices, including input validation and secure coding standards.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Even though we don't collect personal data, we support and respect your privacy rights under various regulations:

Right to Access

You have the right to know what data is collected about you. With OnlineQrs, the answer is simple: we don't collect any data about you.

Right to Deletion

Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to delete. Your information remains under your control on your device.

Right to Correction

Any information you enter into our QR code generator can be corrected by you directly before generating the QR code.

You can download your generated QR codes immediately, giving you complete control over your output.

Compliance with Regulations

Our privacy approach naturally aligns with major privacy regulations including GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), and other global privacy standards. By not collecting data, we eliminate most compliance concerns.
