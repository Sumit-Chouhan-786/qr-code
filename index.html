What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR Code Generator is an online tool that creates Quick Response (QR) codes – two-dimensional barcodes that store various types of information. Unlike traditional barcodes that only store product data, QR codes can contain URLs, contact details, WiFi credentials, payment information, and much more.

How QR Codes Work

QR codes encode information in a matrix of black and white squares readable by smartphone cameras and dedicated scanners. When scanned, the encoded data is instantly processed by the device. Modern smartphones have built-in QR readers in their camera apps, making scanning incredibly convenient.
