What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR Code Generator is an online tool that creates Quick Response (QR) codes – two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information. Unlike traditional barcodes that only store product information, QR codes can contain URLs, contact details, WiFi credentials, payment information, and much more.

How QR Codes Work

QR codes work by encoding information in a matrix of black and white squares that can be read by smartphone cameras and QR code scanners. When scanned, the encoded data is instantly processed and displayed or acted upon by the scanning device. Modern smartphones have built-in QR code readers in their camera apps, making scanning incredibly convenient for users.
